Value Sensitive Design
Designed with intention, not just function.

Autonomy

At the core of this design is the ability for farmers to repair their own equipment without waiting on a technician or outside supplier. If the inner lining is damaged, it can be replaced in seconds, and the outer canvas can be mended using techniques farmers already know. The pivot to a customer-facing design extends this value beyond the farm: once emptied, the bag converts into a reusable tote, giving customers the choice of how to repurpose it rather than dictating a single use.

Preservation

Built into the construction of the grain bag, the biodegradable outer jute canvas and drawstring mechanism create a sealed environment that protects grain from moisture and contamination, preserving the quality of the harvest as it is stored. The wax-coated exterior resists weather and wear over time. The bag's intentional sizing accounts for the expected harvests, ensuring there isn't an excess of product that can go bad.

Sustainability

Rather than a disposable container, the design of the grain bag emphasises sustainability at every stage, from storage to sale to everyday use. Shifting from plastic packaging to jute embraces both a preservation of identity and allows Wild Goose Farms to address changes bound to arise within the next 200 years. When the bag has finally run its course after an estimated five years or longer with care, the jute naturally degrades without damaging the environment.

Cultural Continuity

The grain bags are inexpensive and don't require energy or ongoing costs that would create barriers for the farm long-term. The drawstring cord-lock requires no extra tools or training, and the design fits into existing routines without a learning curve. Beyond the farm, the bag doesn't stop representing Wild Goose Farms when it leaves. As customers carry it as a tote to grocery stores, farmers markets, or everyday errands, the farm's name, logo, and founding date travel with it.

Construction & Details · 04 / 04
Built for repair, not replacement.

Wax-Coated Jute Canvas

Outer layer. Beeswax coating resists moisture. Re-waxable by hand. Research shows up to 35% higher grain germination vs untreated jute.

Overall Buckle Hardware

Strap length adjuster with button clasp. Familiar overall-style mechanism — no learning curve, no tools needed.

Drawstring Cord-Lock

Seals the bag shut with a spring-loaded lock. No ties, no knots. One-handed operation.

Replaceable Inner Lining

Woven polypropylene liner slides out and swaps in seconds. If damaged, replace just the liner — not the whole bag.
